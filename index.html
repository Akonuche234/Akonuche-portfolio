ABOUT ME

Welcome to my website! I'm Emmanuel Akonuche, a Nigerian citizen, a political and public policy analyst.I developed a deep understanding of politics, policy, and the relationship between the two. I'm passionate about using my knowledge to advocate for good governance and education for all, having had firsthand the experience of how poverty and lack of quality education can kill dreams and limit potential. As a result, I founded an initiative titled the "Education is Possible Initiative", targeted at young children from low-income families. As a political analyst, I analyze the effects of current and historical events on society, and as an advocate, I work to inform and empower others to take action. I'm dedicated to creating a more informed and engaged society, this website is a reflection of my interests and values. It's a place where I share my insights and analysis on current events, as well as my thoughts on educational initiatives and good governance. It's also a place where you can find information on how to get involved and make a difference. I believe that we all have a role to play in shaping our future, and this website is my contribution to that effort. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find it useful and inspiring!

MY AGENDA

Over the years, I have engaged over 2,000 young people, and I found out that these young people are brimming with great ideas, creativity, and innovations. But often, their potential is stifled by a lack of opportunity and access to the right resources. These young people are not often heard, and when they are heard, they are not taken seriously. I believe these young people have the potential to create social change, and make positive impact in their local communities, and I believe I can help them to achieve that. I aim to help create a platform where these young ones can advocate for their needs and make their voices heard, through leadership training, mentorship and networking. This is the only way to make our society work and I believe I can be at the forefront to do that,to help put this young generation at the center of national debate. We can make our society work again if we join hands to breed the next generation of young leaders, by creating a formidable platform for them, this is the duty we owe the next generation, this is the duty we owe the society.
